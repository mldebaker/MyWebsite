SEVERE WEATHER DASHBOARD

The task of this assignment was to tell a story with data. The proposal was to create an interactive Severe Weather Dashboard. The data chosen for the project was pulled from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration. In this dashboard you can adjust by clicking on a state or severe weather option. This in turn gives you the total crop damage and total property damage adjusted on those filters.
